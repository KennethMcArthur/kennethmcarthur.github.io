Sword Combats and Mysterious Magics (aka SCAMM) is a full-fledged online multiplayer procedurally-generated open-world role playing game (FOMPORPG) where you can be anyone you want, anytime, anywhere, any.
Join thousands of potential players from all around the world for the most exciting journey of your character's life.
Fight monsters alone or with your friends, or befriend monsters and fight your friends, alone or with your friends, or against them.
But wait: SCAMM is a videogame like no others; its revolutionary features will enhance your gaming experience to a new whole level.

Features


Ultimate Character Customization

The character is everything, it's our projection in the game world, it's ourself! Because of this, being able to customize your character is the fundation of the entire game. But we at FRAUD don't like pre-made characters, they are the embodiment of developers laziness to add more options. Sliders, you say? They are basically pre-made characters, just more of them. We wanted something better, that's why we came up to the idea of a fully customizable character that can be exactly how you want! No sliders, no pre-made options, just the total freedom to add anything to the model. With our system, the character is truly unique because if you are, it must be too.


Truly Unique Loot

Have you ever found the most powerful, legendary sword, just to realize that everyone has the exact same weapon? Has any blacksmith ever forged the exact same weapon twice?
We at FRAUD like unique things to be really unique, this is why we entwined our loot system with the most recent NFT technology. By signing each piece of loot with an unique number from the blockchain, we can assure that no two players will have the same equipment at any given time. Also this means that now pure crafting-based characters can finally have the full time job they always wanted!


Full Player-Driven Quests System

Did you just finished all quests in a zone and are about to leave towards the next?
NOT HAPPENING. In SCAMM you'll NEVER run out of quests.
We at FRAUD think that a fixed questline kills the immersion of a world, the very idea of "finishing a zone" is weird and unnatural.
To address this, we decided to give the power to create quests to the most powerful system in the world: the Players. Generating quests is as easy as thinking: pick something you need to be done, choose a fitting reward and tell everybody in the game that you have a quest for them. You'll make friends REALLY fast!


Full Integrated Compatibility with IoT

We want our player to truly be immersed in the world of SCAMM, at a level where you could interact with the world itself. That's why the game automatically connects to every device that supports Internet of Things in your home and give to each of them a place and a role in SCAMM. Maybe you could warm bread with your IoT toaster to complete a quest, or turn you Smart TV into a remote-view magical device to check your foes' actions. But being in SCAMM world can also be dangerous in many way, monsters lurking in the dark are known to take control of IoT devices and turn them against their owners. You may defeat your wall-clock alone, but better form a group to destroy that fridge!


Limitless World

We wanted the biggest world ever made. Soon we realized that even that wouldn't be big enough because our players deserve way more that that. That's why SCAMM employes a special technology called "EtD" that allows the game to connect the player to every other world ever made. Yes, EVERY world imagined in other games can be accessed from SCAMM for a seamless experience through many different realities trascending even game genres. All you have to do is to access the special portal and click on the "EtD" button and then choose which other world to visit. Possibilities are endless, with SCAMM.
